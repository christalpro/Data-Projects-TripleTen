# Profit Center Analysis

## Overview

The project intent was to act as the consultant to review the superstore’s operations and increase its profitability to avoid bankruptcy.

### Task
1. Identify the important centers of profit and loss for the superstore
2. Identify if advertisment would be useful towards the business
3. Monitor abnormal rates of returned items to the store

### Results

Evaluated profit performance across business units to support strategic financial decision-making.
Developed a profitability dashboard using Tableau to compare revenue, costs, and margins by product category and region
Performed trend analysis and YoY comparisons to identify underperforming profit centers
Designed drill-down views enabling stakeholders to isolate loss areas and optimize resource allocation
Delivered actionable insights that highlighted low-margin segments
