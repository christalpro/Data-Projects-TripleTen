Variables and Definitions
------------------------------------------

1. Product Return Rate
   Description: Average rate each product has been returned

2. Customer Return Rate
   Description: Average rate each customer made a return

3. Profit Center Analysis
   Description: Sub-Category in descending order by sum of profit vs the region in descending order by sum of profit. Used to locate a pattern of the highest and lowest profit centers

4. Profit Analysis Per State
   Description: State vs Category sorted in descending order by sum of profit sorting 

5. Profit by State
Description: Map of states from highest to lowest in SUM of profit. Green showing the highest profit. Red showing the lowest profit.

6. Average Profit Per State
 Description: Highest average profit month per state for Vermont, Indiana, and Washington for advertisement purposes.
Line Chart used just for visuals

7. Highest Profit Months Per State
   Description: Highest average profit month per state for Vermont, Indiana, and Washington for advertisement purposes. 1/5 * highest profit to show 20% of recommended advertising spending.
Square Chart used for visuals instead

***Chart 6 & 7 are pretty much the same charts but with different visuals.


8: Hidden Sheets
Top 5 Products: Filtered data of the top 5 products that had the highest profit
Bottom 5 Products: Filtered data of the bottom 5 products that had the lowest profit

9. Dashboard 1
   Description: All used sheets of date to answer the questions of what are the best products and worse products the company should focus on or stop selling. (Profit Center Analysis, Product Return Rate, Customer Return Rate, Avg Profit Per State, Top 5 Products, Bottom 5 Products)
